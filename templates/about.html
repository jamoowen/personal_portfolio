About me

My name is James and I'm a Software Developer, Data Analyst and amateur boxer. I am passionate about coding and problem solving and love working with new tech. I was born and raised in South Africa, living most of my life there and achieving my University degree there. In early 2021, amidst the pandemic, I decided to pack my bags and move to London (Great timing, I know). It was at first tricky to transition to a different life here in London - with the bipolar weather, rush hour tube traffic and overpriced accomodation. However, despite these cruel aspects of city life, I persevered and have now settled in, almost. I have since worked a few different jobs in London, and competed in many amatuer boxing fights. The last job I was working was as a Trainee Analyst at a top Wealth Management firm. Many of the skills that I learned there are very useful and transferrable. But ultimately, the greatest thing that the position showed me, was how much I truly enjoy coding and working with tech. I obviously ended up leaving that job and have been learning and working as a freelance developer ever since.
That was an incredibly short summary of the last few years of my life. If you would like to have a look at the things I've created please click on the projects tab. Otherwise, please have a look at my CV.
